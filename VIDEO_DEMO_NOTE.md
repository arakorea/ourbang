# Scan page video showroom demo (no real payment)

- scan.html: embeds a local video (assets/videos/ourbanggallery.mp4)
- hotspots: clickable overlays on the video (time-range based)
- click -> modal shows high-res image + info + View & Purchase
- purchase.html: checkout demo page (no payment is processed)

To fine-tune hotspots:
- edit the `hotspots` array inside scan.html (start/end times + x/y/w/h).
